Troubleshoot

What if your computer doesn't recognize your keyboard when you plug it in?

  1. Check to make sure the keyboard is plugged in to USB port correctly
  2. Check to see if you have up to date drivers from keyboard manufacturer
  3. Reboot your computer

What if the keyboard button is stuck and won't stop displaying the character?

  1. Unplug your keyboard from USB port
  2. Make sure your keys can be removed from the exterior of keyboard frame
  3. Get a slot screwdriver or a flat sided tool to lever the key cap off the keyboard frame
  4. Reset the key cap gently back on the keyboard frame
