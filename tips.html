Keyboard Care Tips

  • Unplug and remove keyboard from USB port before cleaning
  • Clean keyboards at least once a month with pressured air
  • If keys can be removed, clean computer keys with warm water and soap
  • Use a Q-tip or toothbrush with sparse amount of water to clean keyboard frame
  • Quickly dry wet areas, if spill occurs
